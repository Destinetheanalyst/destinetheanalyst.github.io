Global Petrol Market Analytics

Performed EDA across 181 countries to model global fuel price behaviour using Linear, Polynomial, and Multi-variable Regression with K-Means clustering to segment markets by pricing and consumption patterns. Key finding: single-variable models show low predictive power for fuel prices — economic drivers (subsidies, taxation, domestic production) require multi-factor modelling. Directly applicable to supply chain cost forecasting for energy-exposed businesses..
